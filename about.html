RPN Stafflink Service Provider Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted manpower outsourcing company, providing reliable and trained staff for corporate offices, institutions, hospitals, and industries. With a strong focus on quality, discipline, and professionalism, we ensure that our clients receive the right workforce to meet their business needs.

We operate with a strong regional presence across India, head office located at Nagpur MH, India. This structure allows us to deliver efficient, localized services while maintaining the professionalism of a unified private limited company.

Our Story

Building success through dedicated workforce solutions

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between businesses and quality manpower, RPN Stafflink has grown from a regional service provider to a pan-India workforce solutions company. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to deliver trained, reliable, and professional staff who become valuable assets to our clients' operations.

Over the years, we have built strong relationships with corporate offices, healthcare institutions, manufacturing facilities, and educational organizations. Our success is measured not just in numbers, but in the long-term partnerships we've established and the thousands of successful placements that have contributed to our clients' growth stories.

Compliance & Legal Standards

Operating with complete transparency and legal adherence

As a registered Private Limited Company, we maintain strict compliance with all labor laws and statutory requirements. Our commitment to legal and ethical operations ensures peace of mind for our clients and fair treatment for our workforce.

  • Legal Registration – Registered under Companies Act with valid licenses and certifications
  • Labor Law Compliance – Full adherence to Contract Labour Act, Minimum Wages Act, and other labor regulations
  • Statutory Benefits – EPF, ESI, and other statutory benefits for all deployed staff
  • Insurance Coverage – Comprehensive insurance for workers and liability coverage
  • Regular Audits – Internal and external audits to ensure compliance and quality
  • Documentation – Proper contracts, agreements, and transparent billing processes
